What should I eat the night before a game?
10:31
Yeah good question. Night before you wanna carb up - think pasta, rice, sweet potato. Nothing fancy, nothing you haven't had before. Game day is NOT the time to try new foods.

Something like chicken pasta with a tomato sauce is perfect. Simple, easy to digest, gets the glycogen topped up.

Make sure you're smashing water all day too - at least 2-3 litres. If you're well hydrated the day before, you're already ahead of most blokes on the park.
